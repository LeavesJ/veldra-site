What we build

Verification infrastructure with observability built in from day one.

✓

Deterministic verdicts

Same template plus same policy always yields the same verdict. Every reject includes a stable reason_code and policy_context.

◆

Mempool-aware fee tiers

Dynamic low, mid, and high tier selection based on real-time mempool tx count. Graceful fallback when mempool is unavailable.

▦

Operator dashboard

Live verdicts, aggregates by reason and tier, policy view, and recent templates. CSV and NDJSON export for compliance.

Policy based template verification for Bitcoin mining pools

Veldra evaluates block templates against operator policy and returns deterministic verdicts with reason codes and context. The dashboard makes rejects explainable instead of mysterious.

Architecture at a glance

Template flows from a producer into a verifier that enforces policy and emits a verdict stream.

Design target: a reject is never “mysterious.” Every reject includes a reason code, the threshold used, and the measured value.

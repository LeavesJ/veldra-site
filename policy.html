Reason codes and policy context

A reject is only credible if it tells you exactly what failed. Veldra emits stable reason codes plus the threshold used and measured values.

Reason codes

Keep these stable
Code Category Description
MIN_AVG_FEE_TOO_LOW fees Average fee rate is below the effective minimum for the selected tier.
MAX_SIGOPS_EXCEEDED consensus Sigops exceeds policy maximum or template violates expected bounds.
MAX_WEIGHT_EXCEEDED consensus Block weight exceeds policy max or ratio constraints.
MEMPOOL_STALE mempool Mempool stats are too old to safely select fee tier thresholds.
POLICY_PARSE_ERROR policy Policy could not be loaded or validated.
Replace these placeholders with your actual reason codes from the verifier. Keep names all caps with underscores and never silently change semantics.

Sample policy.toml

Readable, diffable
# Example only. Use your repo policy schema.

protocol_version = 1

[limits]
max_weight = 4000000
max_sigops = 8000

[fees]
mode = "dynamic_tiers"

[fees.tiers.low]
min_avg_fee_sat_vb = 2.0

[fees.tiers.mid]
min_avg_fee_sat_vb = 8.0

[fees.tiers.high]
min_avg_fee_sat_vb = 20.0
Tier selection input
mempool_size, congestion, staleness
Tier selection output
low | mid | high

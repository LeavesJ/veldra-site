Policy

Reason codes and context

A reject is credible only when it names the failure and shows the numbers. Veldra emits stable codes plus policy context.

Reason codes

Stable names, stable meaning

Code Category Description
MIN_AVG_FEE_TOO_LOW fees Average fee rate is below the effective minimum for the selected tier.
MAX_SIGOPS_EXCEEDED consensus Sigops exceeds policy maximum.
MAX_WEIGHT_EXCEEDED consensus Block weight exceeds policy maximum or ratio constraint.
MEMPOOL_STALE mempool Mempool stats are too old to safely select fee tiers.
POLICY_PARSE_ERROR policy Policy load or validation failed.

Replace this table with your verifier’s actual codes. Keep codes all caps with underscores and avoid semantic drift.

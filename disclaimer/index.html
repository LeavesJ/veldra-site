Mining risk acknowledgment

Bitcoin mining involves inherent technical and financial risks. By using ReserveGrid OS, you acknowledge that template verification is one component of a mining operation and does not guarantee profitability, block production, or revenue. Rejected templates may result in miners receiving no work assignments, which reduces hash utilization. Accepted templates may still produce blocks that the Bitcoin network rejects for reasons outside the scope of policy evaluation (network forks, competing blocks, consensus rule changes).

Consensus safety checks

The consensus safety features (weight ratio, template age, sigops budget, coinbase sigops) are informational checks based on known Bitcoin consensus constants. They do not replace full consensus validation performed by Bitcoin Core or equivalent full node software. A template that passes all ReserveGrid OS safety checks may still be invalid under Bitcoin consensus rules.
